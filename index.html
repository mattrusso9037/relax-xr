The Ritual

Four steps. Ten minutes.
The whole thing.

No menus. No decisions. Just the ritual.

Step 01

Put on your glasses

Tap once. A subtle ambient field fades in. One grounding prompt appears. You select your energy level with your gaze.

Step 02

Breathe with the visuals

Abstract shapes expand and contract with your breath. Slow gradients. No narration overload. Eyes open. You stay in your room.

Step 03

Set your mental direction

One or two affirmations — curated or AI-generated. A visual closure cue replaces the applause. Then silence.

Step 04

Start your day

Visuals fade. One sentence appears briefly. Glasses off. The world is still there — but now you're ready for it.
